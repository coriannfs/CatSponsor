Helping shelter cats live better lives.

Here at CatSponsor, we provide better housing and care for cats while they are in a shelter, awating adoption. They recieve high-quality cat food formulated to give them the best nutrition, have plenty of play time to give them exercise, and have more opportunities to be adopted in good, loving families. Plus, they recieve top-of-the-line veterinary care, so they are always in tip top shape.

However, this care isn't cheap - and while we do the best we can to cover costs, we need help to give these cats their best shot at life. That's where people like you come in - People who care for cats, but may not be able to own one for many reasons, or perhaps you have just the right amount in your home. :)

Through CatSponsor, people can pay a monthly fee to sponsor a shelter cat, or many! You'll recive weekly or Monthly email updates about your sponsored kitties, as well as the inside scoop on our planned events, meet ups, and even more resources in your area where you can further help cats in need. Plus, if you so choose - You can adopt the very cat you sponsor!
